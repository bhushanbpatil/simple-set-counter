Privacy Policy

Simple Set Counter · Last updated: July 8, 2026

Summary

Simple Set Counter is a workout logging app. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your workouts, routines, and settings stay on your iPhone unless you back up your device with iCloud or a computer backup.

Information we collect

We do not collect information from you. The app does not require an account and does not connect to our servers.

Data you enter in the app may include:

  • Exercise names and routines
  • Logged sets (weight, reps, dates)
  • App preferences (units, colors, settings)

This data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage (SwiftData).

How your data is used

Your data is used only to run the app on your device — for example, showing your workout history and progress charts. We cannot access it.

Third-party services

Simple Set Counter does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or third-party SDKs that collect personal data.

Data sharing

We do not share your data with anyone because we do not have access to it.

Data retention & deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the app. Deleting the app removes locally stored workout data from your device.

Children’s privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on GitHub or contact the developer through App Store support for this app.
